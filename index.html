Why KitchenSync?

For anyone who's ever pulled out the chicken too early.

01

No more mental arithmetic

Stop working backwards in your head. If the chicken takes 1h20 and you want to eat at 7, KitchenSync just tells you: start at 17:40. Done.

02

Built for real cooking

No ingredient lists, no method steps. Just the dishes you're juggling and when each one needs to go in. Simple enough to use mid-cook.

03

Stays out of your way

Start cooking and put your phone down. KitchenSync runs quietly in the background and taps your shoulder at exactly the right moment.

04

Your meals, your device

Everything is stored locally. No account, no subscription, no cloud. Your Sunday roast schedule stays between you and your oven.
